DREAMS OR GOALS ?

         • Relation between goals and dreams ?
 - If we define goals vs. dreams from the start, we would be more likely to accomplish our goals and even turn our dreams into tangible results.
 - Don’t let your dreams remain dreams. Make them goals and work on them.
 - Set the success you want to achieve as a goal not a dream.
         • Why? What the difference between them ?
 - Dreams require no actions or plans but goals do.
 - Dreams are aspirational and goals are concrete.
 - Dreams occupy your mind but goals take over your reality.
 - Dreams are seen and goals are achieved.
 - Goals have ends , dreams are endless.
 - Dreams require your imagination and goals require hard work.
